The beginning

Вот мы и в нашем любимом Внуково готовые к отъезду. Прощальный завтрак, пришлось выкинуть чашки и термосы, но завтрак того стоил! И на фото все наши пожиточки: большой чемодан и 2 чемоданчика, сумка с ноутами и сумка с фотиком. На заметку тем, кто летает Победой: убирайте рюкзаки в чемоданы и не платите за ручную кладь :) (у нас рюкзачок вообще складной и переехал в моей сумке).

So, we are at our beloved Vnukovo and ready to depart. Farewell breakfast. We had to throw away our cups and thermoses, but this breakfast totally worth it! On the photo all our belongings: one big suitcase, two small suitcases, one bag with laptops and one bag with photo camera. Good tip if you flying with Pobeda airlines: pack your backpack in your suitcase and don't pay for the hand luggage. (We have a packable backpack and it will be in my bag after the breakfast).

Traditional coffee in Shokoladnitsa before departure. We are in sweatshirts! Now it looks crazy!

Nicely decorated cafe near our. Mesmerizing.

Finally we are in Bergamo. Photo was taken from the plane, so it's a bit bluish-reddish. Flight was awesome! Pobeda airlines are the best ones! The main thing is to take some food with you. But this is the thing we will never forget to do.

As we got outside the plane it's the same feeling we had after arriving at the Podgoritsa: it's crazy hot, very strange trees around and mind blowing mountains!
