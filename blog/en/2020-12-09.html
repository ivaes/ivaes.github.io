Accidental weddings.

Almost in all our trips and travels I stumble upon weddings. Not always I have a possibility to shoot them, but still the collection has accumulated. I’ve decided to gather all these weddings in chronological order. It’s actually interesting to look at different rituals, outfits and venues. And there are a lot of memories from these trips pop up in my head.
